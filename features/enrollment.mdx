Enrollment in SWCE is split into two distinct phases: **inscripción** and **matrícula**. Inscripción is the initial registration step where a new student is formally admitted into a program. Matrícula is the ongoing management of that student's full academic record — their personal data, academic identifiers, documentation, and active enrollments across cuatrimestres. Together, these two phases form the complete lifecycle of a student's presence in the system.

## Inscripción vs. matrícula

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Inscripción" icon="user-plus">
    The first-time enrollment process. Registers a student into a licenciatura, generación, and cuatrimestre, creating the foundational record that links them to the academic program.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Matrícula" icon="id-card">
    Full record management for an already-enrolled student. Covers personal data, academic identifiers, documentation uploads, and editing existing profiles.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Data collected during enrollment

When a student is enrolled, SWCE captures information across four sections.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Personal data (Alumno)" defaultOpen={true}>
    The core identity fields stored for every student:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | `nombre` | First name |
    | `apellido_paterno` | Paternal surname |
    | `apellido_materno` | Maternal surname |
    | `fecha_nacimiento` | Date of birth |
    | `curp` | CURP (18-character national ID, must be unique) |
    | `sexo` | Sex — `M` (male) or `F` (female) |
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Academic data (Datos escolares)">
    Generated or assigned once the student has been inscribed:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | `matricula` | Unique student ID number |
    | `folio` | Administrative folio number (optional) |
    | `foto` | Profile photo |

    The academic record also links the student to their licenciatura, generación, and cuatrimestre through the inscription record.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Contact data (Datos de contacto)">
    Address, phone, and emergency contact information collected as part of the student profile. This information is accessible and editable from the student's matricula record.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Documentation (Documentación)">
    Uploaded supporting documents required for enrollment:

    | Document | Field |
    |---|---|
    | CURP document | `url_curp` |
    | Birth certificate | `url_acta_nacimiento` |
    | Prior studies certificate | `url_certificado_estudios` |

    <Note>
      All three documents are optional in the system, but your institution may require them before finalizing enrollment. Check with your academic coordinator.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Enrollment workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Inscripciones">
    Navigate to **Inscripciones** in the admin sidebar. This screen lists all existing enrollment records and provides the form to register a new student.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the inscription">
    Fill in the student's personal data (nombre, apellidos, CURP, date of birth, sex) and select the target licenciatura, generación, and cuatrimestre. Each combination of student + licenciatura + generación + cuatrimestre must be unique.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the enrollment date">
    Set the `fecha_inscripcion`. This date is recorded on the inscription record and appears on generated documents such as the expediente.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the academic record">
    After the inscription is saved, go to **Matrícula** to assign the student's matricula number, folio, and profile photo under Datos escolares.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload required documents">
    From the student's matricula profile, upload the CURP document, birth certificate, and certificate of prior studies in the Documentación section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the contact record">
    Complete the student's contact information (Datos de contacto) so that the institution has current phone and address details on file.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing a student record

To make changes to an enrolled student's data, go to **Matrícula** and locate the student in the list. Click the edit action to open the student's full profile at:

```
/matricula/editar-alumno/{id}
```

From this screen you can update personal fields, academic identifiers, contact information, and documentation files.

<Warning>
  Changing a student's CURP after initial enrollment will fail if the new value is already registered to another student. CURPs must be unique across all student records.
</Warning>

## Inscription status

Each inscription record carries a `status` flag (active by default). An inactive status indicates the student's enrollment in that specific cuatrimestre has been suspended or closed, without deleting the record.
