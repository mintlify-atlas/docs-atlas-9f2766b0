---
title: "Professor role: teaching and grading"
description: "The Professor portal gives teachers access to their personal profile, assigned class schedule, grade entry for their subjects, and student list exports."
---

Professors have a focused portal designed around their day-to-day teaching responsibilities. After logging in, you land on the professor dashboard, where you can view your schedule, enter grades for the subjects assigned to you, and download documents for your classes.

<Note>
  All pages listed on this page are only accessible to accounts with the **Profesor** role.
</Note>

## Professor dashboard

The professor dashboard at `/panel-profesor` is your starting point. It gives you a summary view of your current assignments and quick links to the rest of the professor portal.

## Profile

Your profile page at `/perfil-profesor` displays your personal information on file with the institution. You can review and update your details directly from this page to keep your record current.

## Schedule

The Schedule page at `/horario-profesor` shows the classes assigned to you for the current term — including subject names, times, and groups. You can also download your schedule as a PDF to keep an offline copy or print it.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="View schedule" icon="calendar-days">
    See all your assigned classes organized by day and time at `/horario-profesor`.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Download schedule PDF" icon="file-pdf">
    Export a printable version of your personal class schedule as a PDF.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Grade entry

The Grade entry page at `/calificaciones-profesor` is where you record grades for the students in your assigned subjects. Grades are entered per subject and saved directly into the system, making them immediately visible to Admins and, once published, to students.

<Tip>
  You can only enter grades for subjects that have been assigned to you by an Admin. If a subject is missing, contact your institution's administrator.
</Tip>

## Student lists

The Student lists page at `/listas-profesor` shows the roster for each of your assigned subjects. From here you can review who is enrolled in each class and export the list as a PDF.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="View student lists" icon="users">

  <Card title="Download student list PDF" icon="file-pdf">
    Export the student roster for a subject as a printable PDF document.
