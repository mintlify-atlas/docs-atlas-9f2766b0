Students have a self-service portal that brings together all the academic information relevant to them. After logging in, you land on the student dashboard, where you can access your schedule, check your grades, and download official documents without needing to visit an administrative office.

<Note>
  All pages listed on this page are only accessible to accounts with the **Estudiante** role.
</Note>

## Student dashboard

The student dashboard at `/panel-estudiante` is your home screen inside SWCE. It gives you an at-a-glance summary of your current academic standing and links to the rest of the student portal.

## Profile

Your profile page at `/mi-perfil` displays your personal and academic information — including your enrollment data and contact details on file with the institution. Review this page to verify that your information is correct, and reach out to an Admin if anything needs to be updated.

From your profile you can also download your **academic record (expediente)** as a PDF, which contains your complete enrollment and academic history.

## Schedule

The Schedule page at `/mi-horario` shows your class schedule for the current term. Each entry lists the subject name, professor, days, and times so you always know where you need to be.

You can download your schedule as a PDF directly from this page for printing or offline reference.

## Grades

The Grades page at `/mis-calificaciones` shows your recorded grades for each subject in the current term. Grades are entered by your professors and reviewed by the institution's administrators.

From the Grades page you can download your **report card (boleta)** as a PDF for a given term, which is an official document showing all your subject grades.

## Downloadable documents

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Report card (Boleta)" icon="file-lines">
    An official grade report for a specific term. Download it from the Grades page at `/mis-calificaciones`.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Schedule PDF (Horario)" icon="calendar">
    A printable version of your current class schedule. Download it from the Schedule page at `/mi-horario`.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Academic record (Expediente)" icon="file-user">
    Your complete academic record with enrollment history. Download it from your Profile page at `/mi-perfil`.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  If a document is unavailable or shows incorrect information, contact your institution's administrator. Students cannot modify their own academic data.
</Tip>
